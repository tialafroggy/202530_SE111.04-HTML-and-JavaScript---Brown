REVIEW

Tshauni Brown

April 21, 2025

Barbie is a clever and heartfelt film that balances satire with genuine emotion. Director Greta Gerwig gives us a whimsical world filled with vibrant visuals and biting social commentary about gender roles and identity.

Margot Robbie shines in the title role, bringing depth and nuance to a character previously seen as one-dimensional. Ryan Gosling's comedic timing as Ken adds charm and levity. Barbie proves that even the most iconic doll can be reimagined into a thought-provoking film.
